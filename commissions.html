Availability and Pricing

I am generally not available for commissions, however I am happy to discuss what you have in mind and if it's a project that particuarlly interests me I might accept the odd piece of commission work. Below is a non-exhaustive list of the types of work I am most versed with:

  • Pet portraits
  • Pet models and sculptures
  • Creature designs
  • Pixel artwork
  • Poseable animal dolls
  • Costume parts

Please note that these are just my main areas of specialism where I have the most experience. I would love to work on new mediums and technologies and I may even offer this at a discounted price if it's something unfamiliar to me. Pricing normally is on a case by case basis, as every piece of artwork I create is so different and completely bespoke. I base my prices off time, materials, and skill involved.
